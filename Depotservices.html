Maintenance and repair

Meeberg provides full repair services for dry box and ISO tank containers. At the depot in Moerdijk, repairs and modification of all sorts are being undertaken daily. This includes repurposing old containers into open top ones and placing baffles in tank containers. The services for tank containers at this depot also includes tracing, where we install electrical heating.
