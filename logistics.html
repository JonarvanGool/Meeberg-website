Logistics

Loading of tank containers

In addition to our transport service for lease or sale tank containers, we can also offer loading of tank containers. This way we can deliver your tank filled and ready for transport.

Shipping/Trucking/Railing

Due to our experience and broad network in the transport sector, Meeberg is able to provide container/tank transport by truck, train or ship across the world. Please contact our Logistics department for information & possibilities.

Door to door service

Our service does not end at the harbour or railway station. We can offer transport to practically every end destination. With the lease or purchase of your equipment we can provide logistical support.
